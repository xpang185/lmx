judge - Evaluate a candidate against a rubric

Usage: judge [OPTIONS] <rubric> [CANDIDATE]

Candidate text can be provided as an argument or piped via stdin.

Arguments:
  <rubric>    Rubric the candidate must satisfy

Options:
  --context <string>    Optional source material
  --positive-example <string>    Example of a candidate that should be MET
  --negative-example <string>    Example of a candidate that should be UNMET
  --model <provider/id>   Override the LLM model
  --temperature <float>   Override temperature
  --verbose               Show runner diagnostics on stderr
  --help                  Show this help text

Tested models:
  openai-codex/gpt-5.5   score: 1.00 (recommended)
  openai-codex/gpt-5.4   score: 1.00
